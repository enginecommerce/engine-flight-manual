Property Types are for adding addiotional information to the product your selling that is not definted by Option Types. For exmaple, you can add the dimensions, color or materal of the product for better organization and search filters.

You can set up Property Types for your store so all your products can have access to them, then adjust their values in the Edit page for a specific product or variant.

Add Property Types to the Store by clicking the link in the Products Menu and create them in a master list.
