Variants are varieties of a product that you can sell.

These varieties are determined by the Option Types you add to your product in the Product Details tab

For your product to have access to these option types, set up the option types in your site through the Option Types link under the Products dropdown menu

To Add Varients you need to:

  1. Set up the Option Types in your Store
  2. Add Option Types to your Product
  3. Attach appropriate values of option types to new variants

To adjust the prices of the individual variants, go to the Prices tab and see the table of Varients. Updating the Variants tab will automatically populate that table.
