General - Basic information about the product.

Images - All images related to the product and opptional images for specific variants.

Variants - Different purchasing options of a master product. These are determined by the Option Types located in the Products Link Menu.

Prices - All pricing information for the product and its variants.

Properties - Adding additional information to the product your selling that is not definted by Option Types. For exmaple, you can add the dimensions, color or materal of the product for better organization and search filters.

Stock - View stock information about a product including Stock Location and Count on Hand.
